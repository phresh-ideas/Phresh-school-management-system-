Our Services

Student Management

  • Admission Management: Streamline student admissions with online application forms, automated shortlisting, and easy student onboarding.
  • Student Information System: Store and manage student data, including demographics, academic history, and medical information.
  • Attendance Tracking: Monitor student attendance, including daily attendance, leave management, and attendance reports.
  • Grade Management: Manage student grades, including assignment grading, exam scores, and grade reports.

Staff Management

  • Staff Information System: Store and manage staff data, including demographics, employment history, and professional development.
  • Attendance Tracking: Monitor staff attendance, including daily attendance, leave management, and attendance reports.
  • Payroll Management: Automate staff payroll, including salary calculations, tax deductions, and payment processing.
  • Performance Management: Evaluate staff performance, including goal setting, performance reviews, and professional development planning.

Exam Management

  • Exam Scheduling: Schedule exams, including creating exam timetables, allocating exam rooms, and managing exam invigilators.
  • Exam Registration: Manage exam registrations, including student registration, exam fee payment, and exam admission ticket generation.
  • Exam Results: Manage exam results, including result entry, result analysis, and result reporting.
  • Exam Analytics: Analyze exam performance, including student performance, subject performance, and exam trends.

Fees Management

  • Fees Structure: Define and manage fees structures, including tuition fees, exam fees, and other charges.
  • Fees Payment: Manage fees payments, including online payment gateways, payment plans, and payment reminders.
  • Fees Reporting: Generate fees reports, including student fees statements, fees paid reports, and fees outstanding reports.
  • Fees Analytics: Analyze fees data, including fees paid, fees outstanding, and fees trends.

Payroll

  • Salary Management: Manage staff salaries, including salary calculations, tax deductions, and payment processing.
  • Payroll Processing: Automate payroll processing, including generating pay slips, paying salaries, and managing payroll taxes.
  • Payroll Reporting: Generate payroll reports, including payroll summaries, pay slip reports, and payroll tax reports.
  • Payroll Analytics: Analyze payroll data, including payroll costs, payroll taxes, and payroll trends.

Library

  • Book Cataloging: Catalog and manage library books, including book titles, authors, publishers, and ISBN numbers.
  • Book Borrowing: Manage book borrowing, including student borrowing, staff borrowing, and book return tracking.
  • Book Reservation: Allow students and staff to reserve books, including book reservation tracking and notification.
  • Library Reporting: Generate library reports, including book inventory reports, borrowing reports, and reservation reports.

Expense

  • Expense Tracking: Track and manage expenses, including student expenses, staff expenses, and school expenses.
  • Expense Reporting: Generate expense reports, including expense summaries, expense details, and expense category reports.
  • Expense Analytics: Analyze expense data, including expense trends, expense categories, and expense totals.
  • Expense Budgeting: Create and manage expense budgets, including budget allocation, budget tracking, and budget reporting.

Budgeting

  • Budget Creation: Create and manage budgets, including income budgets, expense budgets, and capital budgets.
  • Budget Tracking: Track and manage budget performance, including budget variance analysis and budget reporting.
  • Budget Analytics: Analyze budget data, including budget trends, budget categories, and budget totals.
  • Budget Forecasting: Create and manage budget forecasts, including forecasting income, expenses, and capital expenditures.
